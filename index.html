Welcome! I am Seonghan Kwon, an integrated M.A.–Ph.D. student at Korea University and a researcher at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST). 🚀

My work focuses on bridging bio-inspired algorithms and efficient hardware design to build next-generation AI systems. I strive to develop AI technologies that deliver strong performance while operating with low power consumption. 🧠⚡

Ultimately, my goal is to make advanced AI accessible to everyone without limitations — serving as a bridge between intelligent algorithms and practical, energy-efficient implementation. 🌍
